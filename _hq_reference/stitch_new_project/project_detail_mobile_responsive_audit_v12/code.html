Shadows of the Ghats

A journey through the whispers of the Western Ghats.

The Narrative

"The mountains do not speak, they echo. In the stillness of the dawn, we find what we lost in the city noise."

"ಬೆಟ್ಟಗಳು ಮಾತನಾಡುವುದಿಲ್ಲ, ಅವು ಪ್ರತಿಧ್ವನಿಸುತ್ತವೆ. ಮುಂಜಾನೆಯ ನಿಶ್ಯಬ್ದದಲ್ಲಿ, ನಗರದ ಗದ್ದಲದಲ್ಲಿ ನಾವು ಕಳೆದುಕೊಂಡಿದ್ದನ್ನು ಕಂಡುಕೊಳ್ಳುತ್ತೇವೆ."

SC_012 // EXT

Shadows of the Ghats is a visual poem exploring the symbiotic relationship between the tribal communities of the Malnad region and the encroaching modern world. Filmed over 40 days during the peak monsoon, the short captures the raw, unfiltered essence of survival and spirituality.

The decision to shoot in black and white was intentional—to strip away the romanticism of the green landscape and focus on the textures of skin, stone, and mist.
